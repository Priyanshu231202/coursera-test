Picnic Spots

Chill Scenes and Grub Hubs: Perfect Picks for Dilli Ki Sardi!

Sundar Nursery

Immerse yourself in the harmonious blend of history and nature at Sunder Nursery. Originally a 16th-century garden, this heritage park underwent a meticulous restoration, unveiling its charm to the public in 2018. Marvel at Mughal-era monuments, stroll through diverse plant collections, and relax by serene water bodies—a tranquil escape in the city's heart.

Safdarjung Tomb

Seeking a serene and historical ambiance for your picnic? Safdarjung Tomb, surrounded by lush gardens, offers an ideal retreat from the city hustle. The intricate Mughal architecture and well-maintained lawns create a picturesque setting, seamlessly combining leisure with a touch of cultural richness.

Nehru Park

Find your oasis amidst the bustling city at Nehru Park in Delhi. This serene haven, with lush greenery, walking trails, and vibrant flower beds, sets the stage for a delightful picnic. Expansive lawns beckon relaxation, while a centrally located pond adds to the charm, creating a peaceful ambiance for a leisurely picnic experience.

Deer Park

Discover serenity in the heart of Delhi at Deer Park—an urban oasis known for impressive landscapes and diverse flora. Located in the Hauz Khas area, the park is home to various deer, peacocks, and wildlife. Enjoy peaceful walks around water bodies and historic monuments. And if you’re in the mood for some food or a little street shopping, Hauz Khas Village is just a 5-minute walk away!

RHA's Heartfelt Journey:

Changing Lives, One Mission at a Time

2014 - RHA Comes to Life:

• We started small, serving 150 meals. Little did we know, it was just the beginning of something big!

2015 - Mission 100K

• We aimed for the stars and served 98,284 meals across 17 cities. Plus, our first mission with our neighbors in Pakistan - spreading love and meals!

2016 - Mission 500K:

• Bursting with pride, we served 516,916 people across 28 cities. That's when the Robin Hood Academy came to life. Oh, also RHA was invited to TedX!

2017 - MissionMillion:

• Leaving a trail of impact, we served 1,326,658 people across 48 cities. Taking our mission global, one heartfelt meal at a time.

2018 - MissionMillion :

• Huge numbers - 1,988,361 individuals served in 70 diverse cities. And guess what? We made an appearance on Kaun Banega Crorepati! Who would've thought?

2019 - Mission 5:

• Our dedicated team went all out, serving 6,370,000 meals across 112 cities and 160 villages. We went rural, making sure no one slept hungry.

2020 - Mission 30M:

• When the world shut down our robins decided to take up their hands to serve the country, serving 23,250,182 meals across 160 cities. We went on a relentless mission for 45 straight days – because no one should go to bed hungry, pandemic or not!

2021 - Mission 28 States:

• Across 208 cities, we served 5,670,215 meals and vaccinated 55,466 people. We stepped up to ensure a safer community because hunger and health go hand in hand.

2022 - Mission75:

• Providing 7,333,781 meals across 227 cities. We aimed to transform lives in every city – surgeries, admissions, job fairs, mobility vehicles – you name it!

2023 - MissionSwades:

• Making a significant impact, we served 9,235,532 meals across 214 cities and reached 1424 villages. Taking it to the roots, making a heartfelt difference in the heart of India. Because every meal counts and every smile matters.
